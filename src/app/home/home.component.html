About Linda Sports

We look into developing and maintaining partnerships in the global Sports Industry. Taking up major role in Sports Marketing, Consultation and Player Representation in Football. Linda Sports works not only with the established professional Players & Clubs but also provides the required visibility to the hidden talents in the lower divisions.

Our Services

SPORTS MANAGEMENT

We provide sports management services to Committees, clubs, club owners, sports investors, athletes and sports personalities.

We offer the following services in the field of sports management:

  • Sports event production and management;
  • Consulting of rights holders and sports apparel manufacturers;
  • Athlete portfolio management and representation;
  • Notably, we support and develop IT platforms and databases to record, store and analyze practicing and performing of young players in football and other sports.

As a sports management firm, we are committed to a long-term relationship aimed at maximizing of commercial opportunities of our clients. We have an unparalleled industry knowledge and experience in the area of sports business.

SPORTS MARKETING

Our integrated sports consultancy team provides specialized advice and professional services in sports marketing & management, sponsorship/PR/marketing and other niche sports related services. Coupled with our Sports Law practice, we provide unique solutions in the business of sports.

Our services in the field of sports marketing notably include the following:

  • Development and implementation of brand strategies and marketing plans for organizations, sports clubs and athletes;
  • Contracting of sponsors for sports, cultural and social events;
  • Assistance in activation and accompaniment of sponsorship rights;
  • Sports brand development and support;
  • Sports corporate hospitality services.

We work with our clients and partners to bring unmatched experience in the world of sports. Our assistance in sports marketing includes sourcing business opportunities, liaising with rights holders and event organizers, advising on development of a lean marketing structure and sponsorship strategy through multiple formats, as well as assisting with activation and supervising of marketing plan implementation.

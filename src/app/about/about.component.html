About LINDA SPORTS

Established in 2017, Linda Sports is a sports Consulting & Management Agency providing services that are dedicated to the development and management of its clients and athletes careers in football and beyond.

We look into developing and maintaining partnerships in the global Sports Industry. Taking up major role in Sports Marketing, Consultation and Player Representation in Football. Linda Sports works not only with the established professional Players & Clubs but also provides the required visibility to the hidden talents in the lower divisions.

 

We also have a total commitment to honesty and transparency. Not just in business, but with our clients too. So that they can make informed decision based on facts not fantasy.

We provide the highest quality of agency service in order to maximize your earning potential for the duration of your playing career and beyond. We ensure that we can fulfill your needs and secure the very best for you.

We help to enhance the image of our clients in the world of social and digital media. Offering in-house services and through our various specialist partners along with management and assistance with regards to communication with the media.

We offer both legal support and assistance and work with a number of reputable law firms.

Our integrated sports consultancy team provides specialized advice and professional services in sports marketing & management, sponsorship/PR/marketing and other niche sports related services. Coupled with our Sports Law practice, we provide unique solutions in the business of sports.

We provide sports management services to Committees, clubs, club owners, sports investors, athletes and sports personalities.

We represent and manage a diverse roster of athletes. We strive to ensure the long-term success of the athletes. We understand how essential it is for athletes to focus on training while we assist on the commercial aspects of their careers.

We have partners in over 10 countries across the globe, and in Europe these countries include: Spain, Italy, Germany, France, England, Netherlands, and Turkey.

As well as our extensive European connections we have partnerships in Thailand, Malaysia, Argentina, Brazil, Syria, Kyrgyzstan, Colombia and Africa. Which will allow us to contact clubs on your behalf anywhere in the world.
